Our Story

From Bean to Cup, With Love

Brew & Bean Café was born from a simple passion: to create a space where community and coffee come together. Founded in 2018, we started as a small neighborhood café with a big dream. Our journey began when our founder, Sarah, returned from a trip to Colombia with a newfound appreciation for the art of coffee making.

Today, we're proud to be a cornerstone of our community. We work directly with farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala to ensure fair trade practices and the highest quality beans. Every morning, our expert baristas carefully craft each cup, ensuring that your coffee experience is nothing short of exceptional.

Our Values:

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our coffee
  • Community: Building connections, one cup at a time
  • Sustainability: Committed to eco-friendly practices
  • Excellence: Striving for perfection in every detail
